The giant panda is a terrestrial animal and primarily spends its life roaming and feeding in the bamboo forests of the Qinglin Mountains and in the hilly province of Sichuan. Giant pandas are generally solitary. Each adult has a defined territory and a female is not tolerant of other females in her range.
